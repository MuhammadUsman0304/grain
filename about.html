ABOUT US

The KRUZ GRAINS & PULSES story

Kruz Grains and Pulses supplys top quality plant-based Canadian products to buyers across the world.

Our Business Philosophy

Kruz Grains and Pulses philosophy is to partner with our customers and suppliers to supplies a reliable and great product to benefit the food chain, from the farmer to the consumer anywhere in the world with our Canadian grains and pulses.

Headquarters and Operations

Kruz Grains and Pulses headquarter is in Gatineau Quebec, Canada. Our team of highly experinced agricultural specialists are passionate about the plant-based food we bring to the market and commited to the people we serve.

Enhanced Health and Nutrition

Think of it, what if you realize you have been consuming adulterated green peas? You won't like it I bet and if not detected early the effects can be worse later. To protect our clients from such cases we do a thorough background check on our products to ensure that they are safe for consumption. We aim at safeguarding our community health and ensure they thrive from grains and pulses we supply by ensuring they get the best of the produce available.

Whole grains are high in protein, fiber, B vitamins, antioxidants, and trace minerals, among others; magnesium, iron, copper, and zinc. Heart disease, type 2 diabetes, obesity, and various types of cancer are among some of the diseases that have been shown to be significantly reduced by a diet high in whole grains. By purchasing from us you can be assured to get quality grains that will fuel you through the day on consumption without worrying of any side effects since they have been sampled and handled carefully at Kruz Grains and Pulses.

Pulses are abundant in protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals while being low in fat and salt. Pulses aid in the reduction of cholesterol, blood pressure, and blood sugar levels, as well as the prevention of heart disease and weight loss and buying from us guarantees you not to miss out any of these benefits.

Enhanced Environmental Sustainability

Kruz Grains and Pulses deal in the sale of organic, non-GMO and conventional grains and pulses hence you can be assured of consuming organic grains and pulses that have been cultivated in the most ethical manner without any adulteration. Our farming community applies a number of farming techniques to minimize ecological and environmental impact where they have inculcated minimum till farming, crop rotation, and precision agriculture among other practices.

Studies show that pulse crops increases water efficiency, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, enhance soil quality, and have a minimal carbon footprint. By choosing to join our community means that you are helping conserve the environment and contribute to a sustainable future. Being part of our community is therefore more than just satisfying your basic need of food but contributing towards a great course as well, environment conservation.

Enhanced Economic Stability

Grains and pulses provide food security, minimize price volatility, and provide a greater return to producers throughout the world. Pulses are a more economical source of protein in comparison to sea food and animal products hence playing a significant role in addressing the world's expanding population's food demands. Kruz Grains and Pulses as a player in the industry ensures that the value is well distributed in our community by facilitating impeccable deals where you obtain top grains and pulses at the best prices and ensuring producers earn income for their produce.

By purchasing from us then you are fostering all-encompassing economic growth while obtaining the best nutrition at the best rates. On consuming grains and pulses you are further aiding in sustainability by reducing carbon footprint which is another major economic menace facing the world as controlling carbon emissions has proven to be really capital intensive.

Kruz Grains & Pulses works for transparency and sustainability in all sectors and aspires to contribute in as many ways possible to global food security and improved health and nutrition. This is the journey we have chosen to partake and we seek to do the very best to accomplish our goals.
