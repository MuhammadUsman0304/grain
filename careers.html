Experienced Staff

It takes a diverse group of people to make consumers aware of the health beneﬁts and good taste of whole grains. We are always looking for either experienced or graduate staff to work in our firm. We have been working in the agriculture business for more than 35 years, which is why we understand what we need in an employee.


Experienced grains and pulses staff are often hard to find but with our knowledge we are able to source quality agriculture business employees from a variety of backgrounds, those who understand what Kruz Grains and Pulses wants to achieve in the long run.

Why work with us?

Through our extensive involvement in the agriculture business, we are able to recruite people with the skills needed for the current labour market conditions. This helps us make the right choices and at the same time enhance the knowledge and experience of our employees.

Your involvement in our process allows you to build on your skills so that you can have greater success in the future.

Our organization represents the common good of all sectors in the global grains & pulses industry. If you choose to work with us then this is the kind of environment you will be tailored to serve in.

Kruz Grains & Pulses works for transparency and sustainability in all sectors and aspires to contribute in as many ways possible to global food security and improved health and nutrition. These are some of the key values that we seek to instill in our team

Come on board as a team leader, be consistent, focused and you will learn a lot from other team members. One of our greatest quotes is We are a "TEAM" of people who may not be equal in Qualification, Experience or Talent, but are equal in "COMMITMENT" to achieve our goals and success…!!

We seek to inspire and train to be able to serve our customers not just in Canada but the rest of the world. This is what fulfills our mission and vision. Are you a person who wants to achieve such goals? If you are interested in knowing more about Kruz Grains and Pulses or you would like to be our next employee, please phone us at 613-779-2882 or email hr@kruzgrainspulses.com Our recruitment consultants are happy to give you more information on our services and ensure that you are ready to be part of this team.
